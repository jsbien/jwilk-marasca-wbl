{% blocktrans %} This search engine facilitates searching so called Warsaw Dictionary of Polish, available in the DjVu format in the digital library of the University of Warsaw. {%endblocktrans%} {% blocktrans %} As the engine is a modification of the Poliqarp system (developed in the Institute of Computer Science of Polish Academy of Sciences) used to support the National Corpus of Polish, it uses the same query syntax. The modification has been implemented by Jakub Wilk, who also converted the dictionary to the suitable format. {%endblocktrans%} {% blocktrans %} The idea to use Poliqarp for DjVu texts is due to Janusz S. Bień, who presented it in particular in the paper “Facilitating access to digitalized dictionaries”. {%endblocktrans%} {% blocktrans %} The work on the engine is supported by the grant number N N519 384036 of the Ministry of Science and Higher Education. {%endblocktrans%}
